about me

I'm a front-end developer and designer. I take projects from initial sketches through static wireframes and basic prototypes all the way to the live finished product.

I work at TicketBiscuit in Birmigham, Alabama, where I create and update designs for both a public-facing e-commerce platform and a client-facing dashboard. I also design interfaces for our various branded mobile apps on iOS and Android.

My roots are in print design, but these days almost everything is on the web — including my work. I'm increasingly passionate about user experience as the guiding principle for everything I create.
